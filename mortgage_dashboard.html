About This Data

ICE Mortgage Technology (formerly Ellie Mae) operates Encompass®, the loan origination platform used by ~35% of U.S. mortgage lenders. These daily indices reflect actual locked applications — not advertised teaser rates. Published weekdays by ~4 PM ET via the Federal Reserve's FRED database (series OBMMIC30YF & OBMMIFHA30YF). Rates represent what a borrower matching the above profile would have received that day.

Source: Federal Reserve (FRED)  ·  ICE Mortgage Technology  ·  Weekdays ~4 PM ET
Data refreshed weekdays · Open-source via FRED API
